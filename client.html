The Clients

The Daugherty Water for Food Institute is committed to ensuring a water and food secure world without compromising the use of water for other human and environmental needs.

DWFI's approach is to extend the University of Nebraska's expertise through strong partnerships with other universities and public and private sector organizations. Together they are developing research, education and engagement programs in a focused effort to increase food security while ensuring the sustainability of water resources and agricultural systems. DWFI works locally and internationally, bridging the water and agriculture communities and the worlds of small- and large-holder farmers to deliver innovative solutions to this complex global challenge.

Nicholas Brozovic

Nicholas is the Director of Policy at the Daugherty Water for Food Institute, where his research is focused on using economic analysis to understand spatial, dynamic natural resource systems, with a special emphasis on water resources. He designs and evaluates management policies that can maintain or improve the condition of natural resources. He is currently working to establish functioning resource markets, such as groundwater markets, that can be used as both research and teaching platforms and as models of sustainability for industry.

In many regions around the world, there are restrictions on natural resource use. Where it is possible to transfer resource rights, local rules are usually complicated, making transfers time-consuming and costly to organize. To tackle this, Mammoth Trading designs trading platforms that handle the complexity of local physical and regulatory systems in the background and allow people and businesses to exchange resources with ease and clarity. The trading systems maintain anonymity and confidentiality of customer information.

Each platform we build is unique and designed for the customers' specific needs. Mammoth Trading combines economics, physical sciences, and an understanding of regulations in a way that hasn't been done before.

Mammoth Trading currently operate as certified irrigated acreage market in the Twin Platte Natural Resources District in Nebraska, and are working on the development of other systems. They work in the space of water quantity and quality, as well as that of other natural resources.

Richael Young

Richael is the co-founder of Mammoth Trading, Inc., where she serves as the president. Mammoth Trading specializes in building "smart markets", where her team has the technical, legal, and economic expertise necessary to build sophisticated customized online water market platforms.
